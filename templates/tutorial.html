How to Use Our Insurance Calculator

This tutorial will guide you through each step of using our insurance calculator to get accurate premium estimates for your vehicle.

Step 1: Vehicle Information

Select Vehicle Type

Choose your vehicle type from the dropdown menu:

  • Sedan: Standard passenger cars
  • SUV: Sport Utility Vehicles
  • Truck: Pickup trucks and light commercial vehicles
  • Luxury: High-end vehicles

Step 2: Driver Information

Age Group and Driving History

Select your age group and driving history:

  • Age Groups: Different age ranges affect premium rates
  • Driving History: Choose between clean record, minor violations, or accidents
Tip: Being honest about your driving history helps get more accurate estimates.

Step 3: Vehicle Value

Enter Vehicle Value

Input your vehicle's current market value:

  • Use Kelly Blue Book or similar services to determine value
  • Enter amount in whole dollars (no cents)
  • Include modifications if applicable

Step 4: Coverage Selection

Choose Coverage Type

Select your desired coverage level:

  • Basic: Minimum required liability coverage
  • Collision: Adds coverage for accidents
  • Comprehensive: Full coverage including theft, natural disasters, etc.
Note: Higher coverage levels mean better protection but higher premiums.

Step 5: Understanding Results

Premium Estimates

After clicking "Calculate Premium," you'll see:

  • Annual premium estimate
  • Monthly premium breakdown
  • Factors affecting your rate
Pro Tip: Try different coverage combinations to find the best balance of protection and cost.

Additional Tips

  • Use accurate information for best results
  • Consider multiple coverage options
  • Remember these are estimates only
  • Contact insurance providers for official quotes
